Avijit, on a fine cloudy day.

Avijit Ghosh

One fine day, I thought, why not start a blog? I like to write, and what better than to have my thoughts curated on a personal space? So here I am.
Let me begin this journey by introducing myself. I am Avijit Ghosh, an 18 year old teen. I live in Kolkata, India. Besides the regular busy schedule at school, I am a technology freak, and I regularly tinker with Android roms and stuff. Also, it has been almost a year that I have begun making Android apps, which can be found here.
I am enjoying my life. In my free time, I paint, click photos, and write stuff. Since I mostly write small, short essays, I thought, why not here? I could type away in peace, and without the noise and clutter of social networks.
Great ideas are bubbling in my head. I hope this will be a grand new ride for me.
